What's a #YALEFIE?

Think writing an essay on “Deconstructing Heteronormative Taxonomies in Ghana” before drunkenly walking to Toads on a Wednesday night, then Snapchatting, Instagramming, Tweeting, or even Gargling it to your friends. #YALEFIE takes a behind the scenes look at student life at Yale University, where “that Yale thing” might be chugging Redbulls in the library, browsing Facebook at lecture, or complaining about section assholes in your Directed Studies class. #YALEFIE is a fair use parody of The Chainsmokers’ #SELFIE and a final project for Brad Rosen’s CPSC183 (Law, Technology, and Culture).
